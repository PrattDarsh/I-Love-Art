Who We Are

Jhilmil USA – A leader in curating high-quality cultural programs in New Jersey for over two decades. Our experience includes hosting numerous music concerts, dance performances, theater productions, and poetry meets (Kavi Sammelan)

ITV Gold- Under the umbrella of Parikh Worldwide Media is #1 media house in the United States, catering to the Indian and South Asian diaspora.

Together, our vision is to establish New Jersey as the Cultural and Literary Capital of Indian Diaspora in the United States.

Our Objective

Promote Local
Talent

New Jersey is home to a wealth of artistic talent, and we aim to provide them with a stable and vibrant platform. We will also tap on artists from neighboring states.

Bring World-Class
Artists

from India – We will introduce our audience to exceptional artists from India.

Cultivate a Diverse and Dedicated Audience

We aim at building a dedicated and engaged community that seeks and appreciates multiple art forms.

The ‘I Love Arts’ Initiative

Monthly Event Series

Featuring music, dance, theater, literature, and fine arts.

Same Day

Second Sunday of the month, every month

Same Time

4.00-6.00 PM followed by optional dinner

Same Place

ITV Gold Auditorium on Oak Tree Road in Edison, NJ

Involvement

1. We plan to involve youth in organizing and running these programs. We are talking to local High Schools and Universities. We will provide Community Service Certificates to students.

2. At every event we will honor one Community Member with outstanding accomplishments. We will also honor one youth leader (Under 30) at every program.

3. We will provide special assistance to Senior Citizens by offering them discounted tickets and transportation to the venue when possible.

Collaboration & Support

1. We welcome partnerships with organizations serving the Indian diaspora. We leave on them to decide , how they want to help

2. No financial commitment required at this stage.

3. Due recognition will be given to all supporting organizations based on their level of involvement
