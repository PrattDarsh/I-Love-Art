Who We Are

Jhilmil USA

A leader in curating high-quality cultural programs in New Jersey for over two decades. Our experience includes hosting numerous music concerts, dance performances, theater productions, and poetry meets (kavi sammelans).

ITV Gold

Under the umbrella of Parikh Worldwide Media, ITV Gold is #1 media house in the United States. Active for a long time, it caters to the Indian and South Asian diaspora.

Our Vision

To establish New Jersey as the Cultural and Literary Capital of Indian Diaspora in the United States

Our Objective

Promote local talent from the rich pool of artists in New Jersey and neighboring states.

Invite and showcase world-class artists from India.

Cultivate a diverse and dedicated audience that seeks and appreciates multiple art forms

Build an engaged community of art lovers and connoisseur.
